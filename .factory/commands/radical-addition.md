Identify the single smartest, most radically innovative, and most accretive addition you could make to the current plan at this point.

## Steps

1. Briefly restate the plan to ensure alignment.
2. Identify the main constraint or bottleneck.
3. Propose one addition that:
   - Increases long-term leverage or compounding value
   - Expands optionality or asymmetric upside
   - Is realistic within current constraints
4. Briefly justify why this dominates obvious incremental improvements.

## Output Format

```markdown
# Proposed Addition

<Concise description>

## Why This Matters

<Short explanation of leverage and impact>
```
